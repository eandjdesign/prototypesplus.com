Company Biography

Prototypes Plus has been located in Menlo Park, California since the late 1970's. Our 12,000 Sq. Foot facility is extensively equipped with 12 CNC machining centers, multiple CAD/CAM stations, state of the art stereolithography and laser sintering equipment, and full tooling department. We are the largest prototyping company in Northern California and can fulfill a wide range of needs. With our quality, rapid service and competitive pricing, we continue to succeed in building long term relationships with major companies around the globe.



Mission Statement

With experience in the industry, Prototypes Plus is a leader in the product development field. As we continue to advance, we balance technology and human resources to better assist our customers. That balance is the key to our continuous success in providing excellent service and quality. Utilizing our state of the art CNC's, multiple SLA machines, laser sintering equipment and our complete production facility, your next product development program can be successfully accomplished.
